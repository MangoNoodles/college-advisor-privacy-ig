Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 15, 2026

College Advisor ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Quiz Responses: Answers to questionnaire questions about your interests, skills, and preferences to provide personalized major recommendations.
  • Chat Messages: Conversations with our AI assistant to better understand your educational goals.
  • Saved Data: Scholarships, colleges, and programs you save within the app.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide personalized college major recommendations
  • Suggest relevant scholarships and college matches
  • Improve our AI recommendations and app features
  • Respond to your inquiries and provide support

Data Storage and Security

Your quiz responses and preferences are stored locally on your device. When you interact with our AI features, your messages are processed through secure API connections. We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information.

Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

  • Anthropic Claude AI: Powers our AI recommendations. Your conversations are processed according to Anthropic's Privacy Policy.

Children's Privacy

Our app is intended for high school students and parents exploring college options. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the information we have about you
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of data collection by discontinuing use of the app

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
